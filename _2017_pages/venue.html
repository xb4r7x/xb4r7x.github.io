Grand Beach Inn
15,500 sqft • Sleeps 50 • 14 Bedrooms • 11 Bathrooms • 2 Half Bathrooms
Click here for the VRBO page (Photos Available)

Info
14 bedroom, 13 bathroom house with additional on-site 3 bedroom, 2 bath cottage located in the heart of Harbor Country Michigan. The Grand Beach Inn features an outdoor heated swimming pool, fire pit, front and rear porches, recreation room, and family style dinning room. The property is a quick walk to Lake Michigan and nearby tennis courts and golf course. Located just a short drive away is the town of New Buffalo and Warren Dunes State Park.
